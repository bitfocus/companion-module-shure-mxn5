## Shure MXN5

This module will connect to any Shure MXN5-C Networked Loudspeaker.

**Available actions**
* Mute / Unmute
* Gain + / Gain -
* Preset Recall
* Identify Device (Flash Lights)
* Reboot Device

**Available feedbacks**
* Flash State
* Device Audio Mute
* Clip Indicator
* Channel Mute
* Limiter Engaged
